How to Use It Effectively

Ensure you are using a modern browser like Google Chrome or Microsoft Edge (as they possess the most advanced built-in AI speech engines). Connect a decent microphone and minimize background noise. Select your language from the dropdown menu, click "Start Listening," grant microphone permissions, and speak naturally. The AI will type out your sentences in real-time. You can manually edit the text inside the box anytime to fix minor punctuation before clicking "Copy Text".
