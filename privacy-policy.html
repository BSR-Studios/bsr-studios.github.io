Privacy Policy

Transparency is our priority. Learn exactly how we protect your data while you use our website, AI tools, and desktop software.

Last Updated: March 18, 2026

At BSR Studios (accessible from https://bsr-studios.github.io/), one of our main priorities is the privacy of our visitors and users. This Privacy Policy document contains types of information that is collected and recorded by BSR Studios and how we use it. This Privacy Policy applies to our website, our web-based AI tools, and our downloadable Windows desktop applications (including AnyWebWall, AnyVidWall, and Any Screen Recorder).

If you have additional questions or require more information about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us.

1. Information We Collect

We believe in data minimization. We only collect the information absolutely necessary to provide you with a smooth and secure experience.

A. Personal Information

If you contact us directly (via our Web3Forms contact page), we may receive additional information about you such as your name, email address, the contents of the message and/or attachments you may send us, and any other information you may choose to provide. We do not sell or share this information with third parties.

B. Log Files and Analytics

BSR Studios follows a standard procedure of using log files. These files log visitors when they visit websites. The information collected by log files includes internet protocol (IP) addresses, browser type, Internet Service Provider (ISP), date and time stamp, referring/exit pages, and possibly the number of clicks. These are not linked to any information that is personally identifiable. We use Google Analytics to analyze trends, administer the site, and track user movement on the website to improve user experience.

C. Software Telemetry & Downloads

Our downloadable desktop applications are built with a strict "Performance First" and "Anti-Bloatware" philosophy. Our Windows applications (AnyWebWall, Any Screen Recorder, etc.) operate locally on your machine. We do not integrate hidden background telemetry, keyloggers, or invasive tracking scripts into our executables. However, when you click a download link on our website, we use a secure backend API (Supabase & Vercel) strictly to increment and display the public "Download Counter" for that specific app. No personal hardware data is linked to this download count.

2. Web-Based AI Tools Privacy

BSR Studios hosts web-based Artificial Intelligence tools, including our AI Voice Studio. When you use these tools:

  • Text Processing: The text you input into our AI generators is sent securely to our backend API solely for the purpose of generating the requested audio or visual file.
  • No Permanent Storage: We do not permanently store, log, or train future AI models on the personal scripts, texts, or prompts you submit through our free public tools. Once the file is generated and sent back to your browser, the data is flushed from our active processing servers.

3. Cookies and Web Beacons

Like any other website, BSR Studios uses "cookies". These cookies are used to store information including visitors' preferences, and the pages on the website that the visitor accessed or visited. The information is used to optimize the users' experience by customizing our web page content based on visitors' browser type and/or other information.

4. Google DoubleClick DART Cookie & Advertising

Google is one of a third-party vendor on our site. It also uses cookies, known as DART cookies, to serve ads to our site visitors based upon their visit to our site and other sites on the internet. However, visitors may choose to decline the use of DART cookies by visiting the Google ad and content network Privacy Policy at the following URL – https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads

Some of advertisers on our site may use cookies and web beacons. Our advertising partners are listed below. Each of our advertising partners has their own Privacy Policy for their policies on user data.

  • Google AdSense: We may use Google AdSense in the future to display ads on some pages of this website. These third-party ad servers use technologies like cookies, JavaScript, or Web Beacons that are sent directly to your browser. They automatically receive your IP address when this occurs. These technologies are used to measure the effectiveness of their advertising campaigns and/or to personalize the advertising content that you see.

Note: BSR Studios has no access to or control over these cookies that are used by third-party advertisers.

5. CCPA Privacy Rights (Do Not Sell My Personal Information)

Under the CCPA, among other rights, California consumers have the right to:

  • Request that a business that collects a consumer's personal data disclose the categories and specific pieces of personal data that a business has collected about consumers.
  • Request that a business delete any personal data about the consumer that a business has collected.
  • Request that a business that sells a consumer's personal data, not sell the consumer's personal data.

If you make a request, we have one month to respond to you. If you would like to exercise any of these rights, please contact us.

6. GDPR Data Protection Rights

We would like to make sure you are fully aware of all of your data protection rights. Every user is entitled to the following:

  • The right to access – You have the right to request copies of your personal data.
  • The right to rectification – You have the right to request that we correct any information you believe is inaccurate.
  • The right to erasure – You have the right to request that we erase your personal data, under certain conditions.
  • The right to restrict processing – You have the right to request that we restrict the processing of your personal data, under certain conditions.

7. Children's Information (COPPA)

Another part of our priority is adding protection for children while using the internet. We encourage parents and guardians to observe, participate in, and/or monitor and guide their online activity. BSR Studios does not knowingly collect any Personal Identifiable Information from children under the age of 13. If you think that your child provided this kind of information on our website, we strongly encourage you to contact us immediately and we will do our best efforts to promptly remove such information from our records.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Thus, we advise you to review this page periodically for any changes. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. These changes are effective immediately, after they are posted on this page.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us by visiting our Contact Page.
