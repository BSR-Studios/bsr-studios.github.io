How We Can Help You

At BSR Studios, our priority is ensuring that our users have a seamless, lag-free experience across all our desktop applications. Whether you are using Any Screen Recorder for gaming content, or AnyWebWall for your aesthetic desktop setup, we are here to support you.

  • Bug Reports & Glitches: Did a software crash or an .aww file fail to load? Let us know. Please include your PC specifications (RAM, Processor, Windows Version) in the message box to help us diagnose the issue faster.
  • Feature Requests: Our software evolves based on community feedback. If you want a specific feature added to AnyVidWall or need a new type of interactive wallpaper created, send us your ideas!
  • Business Inquiries: For partnerships, software collaborations, or open-source repository queries, please use the subject line "Business Inquiry".

Response Time: Because we are an independent development studio, our standard response time is between 24 to 48 hours. We read every single email we receive and appreciate your patience.
