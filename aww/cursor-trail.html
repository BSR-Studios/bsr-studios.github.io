The Science Behind Zero-Lag Interactivity

The biggest problem with most interactive desktop backgrounds is that they consume heavy processing power. Calculating fluid dynamics and mouse tracking on a high-resolution display usually requires a web framework, which causes massive FPS lag during gaming or heavy workloads. At BSR Studios, we solved this issue by utilizing the AnyWebWall native C++ and OpenGL engine.
