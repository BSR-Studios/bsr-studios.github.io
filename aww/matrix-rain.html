Many users attempt to achieve this look by downloading an MP4 video of the Matrix rain from YouTube and looping it using standard wallpaper software. If you are using a low-end laptop with 4GB or 8GB of RAM, running a video wallpaper in the background will drain your battery rapidly and cause severe frame drops (FPS lag) when you try to play video games.
