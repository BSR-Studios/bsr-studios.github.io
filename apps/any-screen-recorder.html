About Any Screen Recorder

Any Screen Recorder is an ultra-lightweight, highly optimized screen capture software designed specifically for gamers, creators, and professionals with low-end or mid-range PCs.

Tired of laggy recordings and massive FPS drops? We solve this by using a custom Direct3D and WIC (Windows Imaging Component) engine to capture directly from your GPU. Our revolutionary "Smart Delta Frame" logic detects when your screen is idle, dropping CPU usage to a mere 2% to 3%. To save system performance, it creates a raw .screc file in the background during gameplay. When you are done, you can convert it into a buttery-smooth MP4 video with a single click. 100% free, no annoying watermarks, and zero time limits!
