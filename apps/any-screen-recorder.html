The "Zero-Lag" Secret

Traditional screen recorders like OBS Studio or Bandicam encode and compress your video while you are recording. This requires massive CPU and RAM power, causing your game FPS to drop drastically on a low-end PC.

Any Screen Recorder completely changes the game. We use a revolutionary "Smart Delta Frame" logic. During recording, the app does not compress video. Instead, it captures raw data directly from your GPU at just 2% to 3% CPU usage and creates a temporary .screc file. Only after you stop recording and close your game, the software takes its time to compile that raw data into a beautiful, buttery-smooth MP4 video. This guarantees 0% performance loss while you play!

⚡ Zero-Lag Architecture

Separates video rendering from screen capture to ensure absolute zero PC hang or game lag.

🎮 Crystal Clear Audio

How to Use Any Screen Recorder

  • Configure Settings: Launch the app. Select your desired Resolution (720p, 1080p, or Fullscreen) and choose your framerate (30 FPS or 60 FPS).
  • Additional Options: Toggle the "Show Cursor" or "Highlight Cursor" boxes if you are recording a tutorial. You can also toggle "Record System Audio" and choose your preferred app theme (Light/Dark).
  • Set Save Location: Click the "Browse" button to select exactly where you want your final videos to be saved.
  • Start Recording: Click the big "Start Recording" button. The app will immediately hide itself and a small recording icon will appear in your system tray (bottom right).
  • Stop Recording: When you are finished, click the icon in the system tray to stop. The raw .screc file will now appear in the "Pending Raw Files" section.
  • Convert to Video: Select the pending raw file and click "Turn into Video". The app will now safely compile the raw data into a highly compressed MP4 video!

Frequently Asked Questions

1. Can this run smoothly on a low-end PC?
Yes! Because it records uncompressed raw data directly from the GPU, it runs like butter on low-end systems without causing game lag.
2. Does it support Facecam (Webcam) recording?
No, currently the software only focuses on capturing high-performance screen data and internal system audio.
3. Does it leave a watermark on the video?
Absolutely not. The software is 100% free and leaves zero watermarks on your final MP4 file.
4. Is there any time limit on recordings?
No, there is no time limit. You can record for as long as you want, provided you have enough storage space on your hard drive.

Known Limitations

  • Large Raw Files: Because it saves uncompressed data to prevent lag, a 1-minute .screc raw file can be 70MB to 80MB. (Don't worry, once you click "Turn into Video", the final MP4 will be compressed down to just 2-3MB).
  • Storage Speed: For 60FPS recording, your hard drive needs decent Write speeds (7-8 MB/s). Using an SSD is highly recommended to prevent dropped frames.

Troubleshooting

Raw File Not Compiling? If the software seems stuck while clicking "Turn into Video", ensure you have enough free storage space on your PC to save the new MP4 file.
Audio Not Recording? Ensure that "Record System Audio" is checked before you hit Start. If it still fails, check your Windows Sound settings and ensure your default output device is active.
