The Best Free Alternative to Wallpaper Engine

Looking for a live wallpaper app that doesn't lag? AnyVidWall is engineered for performance. Unlike other heavy wallpaper engines that eat up your RAM, AnyVidWall is designed specifically for Windows 7, 10, and 11 on low-spec hardware.

You can set any video file (MP4, MKV, AVI) as your permanent desktop background. The best part is our "Smart Gaming Mode" — the wallpaper automatically pauses when you launch full-screen games like GTA V, Valorant, or CS:GO, giving you maximum FPS without closing the app. It's the perfect solution for gamers who want a cool setup without sacrificing performance.

Key Features:

  • Extremely Low CPU & RAM Usage
  • Supports MP4, MKV, AVI and WebM formats
  • Auto-Pause when gaming (Zero Lag)
  • Select any video file from your PC easily
  • Clean and Simple Interface
